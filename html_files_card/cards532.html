Welcome to 5-3-2 Card Game

5-3-2 is a trick-taking card game for 3 players, played with a standard 52-card deck. Each player receives 10 cards. The game features a randomly chosen trump suit, and the objective is to win the majority of 10 rounds by playing the highest card in each trick. Trump cards beat all others, and the winner of each round leads the next.

The player who first wins 5 rounds, or the most rounds after 10 tricks, is declared the winner.
Rules
  • 3 players, 10 cards each.
  • Random player chooses the trump suit (♠, ♥, ♦, ♣).
  • Win 5 out of 10 rounds to win the game.
  • Each round, players play one card in order; the first card determines the lead suit.
  • Players must follow the lead suit if possible; otherwise, play any card.
  • Trump cards beat non-trump cards; highest card of the lead suit wins if no trump is played.
  • Round winner plays first in the next round.
  • If no player wins 5 rounds, the player(s) with the most rounds won after 10 rounds is/are the winner(s).
