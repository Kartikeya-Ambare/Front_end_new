Welcome to the Bluff Game

Here are the rules
Game Flow
  • Your Turn: Look at your hand organized by rank
  • Declare Rank: Choose what rank you claim to be playing
  • Select Cards: Pick which cards from your hand to play
  • Challenge Phase: The next player can challenge your claim
  • Resolution: If challenged, cards are revealed
Key Rules
  • First Turn: Any player can start with any rank
  • Following Turns: Must play the same rank OR pass
  • Passing: If everyone passes, the rank resets
  • Challenging: Next player can challenge if they suspect a bluff
  • Bluff Detected: Bluffer takes all cards in the center pile
  • No Bluff: Challenger takes all cards in the center pile
